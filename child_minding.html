Child Minding Course

6 Weeks Short Course Certificate Program

Professional childcare training for modern childcare services

Course Overview

This comprehensive child minding course provides you with the essential skills and knowledge to provide professional childcare services. Learn about child development, safety procedures, and engaging activities for children of different ages.

What You'll Learn

  • Child development stages and milestones
  • Safety and emergency procedures
  • Nutrition and meal planning for children
  • Engaging educational activities
  • Behavior management techniques
  • First aid for common childhood injuries
  • Communication with parents

Career Opportunities

  • Professional Child Minder
  • Daycare Assistant
  • Nanny
  • After-school Care Provider
  • Childcare Entrepreneur
