Garden Maintenance Course

6 Weeks Short Course Practical Training

Professional gardening techniques and landscape maintenance skills

Course Overview

Learn professional garden maintenance skills including plant care, lawn maintenance, irrigation systems, and landscape upkeep. This hands-on course prepares you for a career in garden maintenance and landscaping services.

What You'll Learn

  • Plant identification and care requirements
  • Lawn maintenance and turf management
  • Pruning and trimming techniques
  • Weed and pest control methods
  • Irrigation system maintenance
  • Seasonal garden care
  • Tool maintenance and safety

Career Opportunities

  • Garden Maintenance Specialist
  • Landscape Maintenance Worker
  • Groundskeeper
  • Estate Gardener
  • Garden Service Entrepreneur

Practical Training

  • Hands-on garden maintenance projects
  • Real-world client scenarios
  • Tool and equipment training
  • Business management basics
