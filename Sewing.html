Sewing Course

6 Months Comprehensive Training Portfolio Development

Complete garment construction, alteration, and textile skills training

Course Overview

Master the art of sewing from basic stitches to advanced garment construction. This comprehensive course covers everything from operating sewing machines to creating custom clothing and running a sewing business. Learn professional techniques that can lead to employment or entrepreneurship opportunities.

What You'll Learn

Basic Sewing Skills

  • Machine operation and maintenance
  • Hand stitching techniques
  • Fabric selection and preparation
  • Pattern reading and cutting

Garment Construction

  • Dressmaking and tailoring
  • Clothing alteration and repair
  • Professional finishing techniques
  • Custom fitting and measurements

Specialized Techniques

  • Embroidery and decorative stitching
  • Quilting and patchwork
  • Home decor sewing
  • Accessory creation

Business Skills

  • Costing and pricing
  • Client consultation
  • Portfolio development
  • Small business management

Career Opportunities

  • Professional Seamstress/Tailor
  • Alterations Specialist
  • Fashion Production Assistant
  • Costume Maker
  • Sewing Instructor
  • Textile Entrepreneur
  • Custom Clothing Designer

Course Projects

  • Create a complete garment from scratch
  • Professional alteration of existing clothing
  • Custom home decor items
  • Fashion accessories portfolio
  • Business plan for sewing enterprise

Equipment & Materials

  • All sewing machines provided during training
  • Basic sewing kit included
  • Fabric and materials for practice projects
  • Professional tools and equipment access
  • Patterns and instructional materials
