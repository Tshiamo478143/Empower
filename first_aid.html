First Aid Course

6 Months Comprehensive Training Certified Program

Comprehensive emergency response and life-saving skills training

Course Overview

This comprehensive first aid course provides you with essential life-saving skills and emergency response training. Learn to handle medical emergencies confidently and professionally in various settings.

What You'll Learn

  • CPR and resuscitation techniques
  • Emergency response procedures
  • Wound care and bandaging
  • Fracture and sprain management
  • Emergency childbirth procedures
  • Poisoning and overdose response
  • Emergency equipment operation

Career Opportunities

  • Certified First Aid Responder
  • Workplace Safety Officer
  • Community Health Worker
  • Event Medical Staff
  • First Aid Trainer

Certification

  • Level 3 First Aid Certification
  • CPR and AED Certification
  • Emergency Response Certificate
  • Workplace Safety Compliance
