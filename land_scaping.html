Land Scaping Course

6 Months Professional Training Portfolio Development

Professional landscape design, installation, and maintenance techniques

Course Overview

Learn the art and science of landscape design and installation. This comprehensive course covers everything from conceptual design to practical implementation of beautiful, functional outdoor spaces.

What You'll Learn

  • Landscape design principles and elements
  • Plant selection and placement
  • Hardscape installation (paths, walls, patios)
  • Irrigation system design and installation
  • Grading and drainage solutions
  • Project management and costing
  • Client consultation and presentation

Career Opportunities

  • Landscape Designer
  • Landscape Contractor
  • Garden Designer
  • Landscape Project Manager
  • Landscaping Business Owner

Practical Projects

  • Residential garden design
  • Commercial landscape planning
  • Installation of hardscape features
  • Planting design implementation
  • Portfolio development
