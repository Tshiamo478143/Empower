Life Skills Course

6 Months Personal Development Career Preparation

Essential personal and professional development skills for career growth and life success

Course Overview

This comprehensive life skills course is designed to equip you with essential personal and professional competencies needed for success in both career and personal life. Learn practical skills that enhance employability, improve relationships, and build confidence for lifelong success.

Core Skill Areas

Communication Skills

  • Effective verbal and written communication
  • Active listening techniques
  • Public speaking and presentation skills
  • Conflict resolution and negotiation
  • Professional email and phone etiquette

Financial Literacy

  • Personal budgeting and money management
  • Banking and savings strategies
  • Understanding credit and loans
  • Basic investment principles
  • Financial goal setting

Career Development

  • CV writing and job application skills
  • Interview preparation and techniques
  • Professional networking
  • Workplace ethics and conduct
  • Career planning and advancement

Personal Management

  • Time management and organization
  • Goal setting and achievement
  • Stress management techniques
  • Problem-solving and decision making
  • Self-confidence building

Digital Literacy

  • Basic computer skills
  • Internet and email proficiency
  • Microsoft Office applications
  • Online safety and security
  • Social media for professional use

Health & Wellness

  • Basic health and nutrition
  • Physical fitness fundamentals
  • Mental health awareness
  • Healthy relationship building
  • Work-life balance strategies

Career Opportunities

  • Office Administrator
  • Customer Service Representative
  • Retail Supervisor
  • Team Leader positions
  • Entry-level management roles
  • Community Outreach Worker
  • Various service industry positions

Course Benefits

  • Increased employability across multiple industries
  • Improved confidence in professional settings
  • Better financial management and independence
  • Enhanced personal relationships
  • Stronger problem-solving abilities
  • Certificate of completion for job applications
  • Ongoing career support and guidance

Practical Learning Approach

  • Interactive workshops and role-playing exercises
  • Real-world scenario simulations
  • Personal development projects
  • One-on-one coaching sessions
  • Group discussions and peer learning
  • Career portfolio development
  • Mock interviews and assessment centers

Who Should Attend

  • School leavers entering the workforce
  • Individuals seeking career changes
  • People returning to work after a break
  • Anyone wanting to improve life management skills
  • Individuals preparing for leadership roles
  • Those seeking personal growth and development
